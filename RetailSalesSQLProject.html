Retail Sales SQL Project

Business Task and Overview:

The primary task was to create an efficient system for exploring and analyzing retail sales data to support decision-making. This involved setting up a reliable database, cleaning the data for accuracy, and using SQL to perform exploratory data analysis (EDA). The goal was to answer key business questions related to sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance, which provided valuable insights that could guide strategic planning and optimize retail operations.

Insights:

  1. Customer Demographics:
    2. This dataset includes customers from various age groups, with sales distributed across three different categories: Electronics, Clothing, Beauty.
  2. High-Value Transactions:
    3. Just over 300 sales had a total sale amount greater than $1,000.00.
  3. Sales Trends:
    4. Monthly analysis shows sales variations, helping identify peak seasons. 2022 had its largest number of average sales in July, while 2023's highest was February.
  4. Customer Insights
    5. Our analysis revealed that our top 5 customers were: Customer_id 3, with $38,440.00 in total sales, Customer_id 1 with $30,750.00 in total sales, Customer_id 5 with $30,405.00 in total sales, Customer_id 2 with $25,295 in total sales, and Customer_id 4 with $23,580.00 in total sales.
